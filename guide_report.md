# India Travel Guide
## Accommodations
The cost of accommodations in India varies depending on the location and type of accommodation. On average, a 3-star hotel costs ₹2,500 to ₹5,000 (approximately 25-50€) per night. Budget-friendly options include hostels and budget hotels.

## Transportation
The most efficient way to travel in India is by train. Trains are affordable and can take you to many cities. Taxis and buses are also available but may be less reliable. For long trips, consider flying.

## Food
Indian cuisine is diverse and delicious. Be sure to try local specialties such as curries, naan bread, and biryani. Street food is also popular and affordable.

### Attractions

* **Taj Mahal** (Agra): A stunning white marble mausoleum and one of the Seven Wonders of the World.
* **Red Fort** (Delhi): A historic fort with beautiful architecture and a glimpse into India's past.
* **Golden Temple** (Amritsar): A sacred gurudwara (Sikh temple) with stunning architecture and serene surroundings.

### Events

* **Diwali Festival of Lights**: A five-day festival celebrating the triumph of light over darkness, usually in November.
* **Holi Colors Festival**: A vibrant celebration of colors and spring, typically in March.
* **Navratri Garba Festival**: A nine-night festival celebrating the divine feminine energy, usually in September or October.

### Activities
* **Go on a Spice Market Tour** (Mumbai): Explore the bustling markets filled with exotic spices, herbs, and other ingredients used in Indian cuisine.
* **Take a Cooking Class** (Bangalore): Learn how to prepare traditional Indian dishes like curries, tandoori chicken, and naan bread.
* **Visit a Yoga or Meditation Retreat** (Rishikesh): Relax and rejuvenate in the spiritual heart of India.

## Itinerary
### Day 1-2: Delhi

* Arrive in New Delhi on February 1st.
* Explore the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and Jama Masjid.
* Visit the iconic India Gate and take a stroll around the Lodhi Gardens.

### Day 3-4: Agra

* Take an overnight train to Agra (approximately 3 hours).
* Visit the Taj Mahal at sunrise.
* Explore the Red Fort and Fatehpur Sikri.

### Day 5-6: Varanasi

* Fly from Agra to Varanasi (approximately 1 hour).
* Explore the ghats along the Ganges River, including Dasaswamedh Ghat and Kesar Bagh Ghat.
* Take a sunrise boat ride to witness the city's vibrant spiritual scene.

### Day 7-8: Rishikesh

* Fly from Varanasi to Rishikesh (approximately 1 hour).
* Visit the Yoga and Meditation Retreat for rejuvenation.
* Explore the temples, ashrams, and scenic beauty of Rishikesh.

### Day 9-10: Mumbai

* Take a flight from Rishikesh to Mumbai (approximately 2 hours).
* Explore the street food scene at Colaba Causeway.
* Visit the iconic Gateway of India and take a stroll along Marine Drive.

### Day 11-12: Goa

* Fly from Mumbai to Goa (approximately 1 hour).
* Relax on the beaches, such as Palolem Beach or Baga Beach.
* Take a sunset cruise to witness the stunning natural beauty of Goa.

### Departure
Depart from Goa on February 28th.
